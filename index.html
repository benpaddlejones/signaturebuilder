Customise your email signature

Instructions

  1. Make sure you are using your desktop computer, not a mobile or tablet. Internet Explorer is the preferred browser for performing this task.
  2. Customise your signature with the above form click 'Show School Settings' to change school details
  3. Click on this link and sign in to open your options page in a different tab or log into your email and click on the black cog next to your neam in the top right corner, then choose "Options" from the menu.
  4. Choose the "settings" option in the left menu
  5. Delete your existing signature this must be done before the next step
  6. Return to this tab in your browser
  7. Click the "Copy for Web Outlook" button
  8. Select the browser tab with your email settings page open
  9. Click into the "Email Signature Box" so the cursor can be seen flashing
  10. Paste (PC: Ctrl + V) (Mac: ⌘ + V)
  11. Click the "Save" button.
